Privacy Notice for Data Donation Platform

If you have questions, contact rse@aalto.fi.

You have been invited to participate in a research study. Participation in this study is voluntary. You can discontinue your participation at any time without negative consequences. This privacy notice describes the use of your data.

  1. What personal data is processed?

    Data donated through this platform may include data from third-party services such as Google (search history, YouTube history, app usage) and TikTok (activity data). The specific data types collected depend on the study configuration.

  2. How is personal data minimized?

    • Only data types specified by the study are collected.
    • Date ranges can be restricted to the study period.
    • During analysis, all data is processed without identifiers.

  3. Legal basis for processing personal data

    The processing of personal data is required for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest, namely scientific research.

  4. Sharing of personal data

    Aalto University may share research data with third-party collaborators who are processors of your personal data. These collaborators will NOT have access to your name or contact information. Data shared with collaborators will not be directly identifiable to you.

  5. Transfer of personal data to non-EU/EEA countries

    Personal data is not transferred to non-EU/EEA countries.

  6. Storage period and anonymisation

    Research data is stored such that it can be completely separated from your identifiers. Research data is stored for five years after the end of the active phase of the study.

  7. Rights of the study participant

    According to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right:

    • to obtain information on the processing of your personal data
    • of access to your data
    • to request rectification of your data
    • to request restricting the processing of your data
    • to object to the processing of your data
    • to request erasure of your data

    To exercise your rights, contact the Data Protection Officer of Aalto University.

  8. Contact information

    Data Protection Officer: dpo@aalto.fi

    Data requests: datarequest.aalto.fi

    If you believe your data has been processed in violation of the GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).
