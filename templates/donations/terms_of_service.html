Terms of Service

Introduction

This data donation service facilitates research data donation from participants to Aalto University researchers. By using this service, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

Participants understand their data will be used for academic research as described in the Privacy Notice.

Researchers agree to use the donated data solely for research purposes and in accordance with legal and ethical guidelines, following research plans pre-reviewed by the Aalto University research ethics committee.

This service is a data processing pipeline: it downloads data you authorize from third-party platforms (such as Google and TikTok) using their official data portability APIs, processes it according to the study configuration, and makes it available to the researcher. It is not a long-term data store.

Eligibility

This service is available to participants in research studies conducted by Aalto University researchers.

If you are under the age of 15, you cannot use this service unless a guardian has separately reviewed the consent with the researchers.

Data Use and Restrictions

Permitted Uses: Researchers may use participant data solely for academic research as stated in the study's research plan.

Prohibited Uses: Researchers may not commercially exploit, sell, or redistribute participant personal data, or use the data for any purpose beyond those stated in the research plan.

Data Storage & Retention

All donated data and OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. The service is hosted on servers managed by Aalto University with access restricted to authorized personnel.

Data is retained on this service only as long as needed for processing and researcher download. Your donated data may contain identifying information; this service does not remove identifiers. It is the researcher's responsibility to handle identifiers appropriately according to their study's data management plan.

Data retention periods and deletion procedures are described in the Privacy Notice.

Participant Withdrawal

Participants may revoke a donation at any time via their donation page. Revocation immediately deletes all associated data and OAuth tokens from this service and revokes OAuth access to the third-party platform.

Researchers may have downloaded data prior to revocation. Retention and deletion of downloaded copies is governed by the study's data management plan.

Researcher Obligations

Researchers agree to process data only in accordance with research plans reviewed by the Aalto University research ethics committee, and to cooperate with institutional oversight and data auditing. Researchers must comply with all applicable data protection regulations, including GDPR and Finnish data protection law.

Compliance and Enforcement

Researchers must adhere to these terms and to applicable legal and research integrity requirements. Violations may result in revocation of access or termination of data collection.

Contact Information

Contact Aalto RSEs (rse@aalto.fi) for more information or assistance regarding these terms of service.
